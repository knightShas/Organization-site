Voyage Educare Foundation is a not for profit company registered as Section 8 company under Companies Act, 2013. Our primary objective is to revolutionize education and healthcare services for the underprivileged rural & urban communities in India. We believe in the impact of holistic education with care towards transforming underserved rural and urban communities into a sustainable global equitable society. We fulfil the organizational objectives through our various initiatives and programs.
